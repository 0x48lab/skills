=== Skills Guide ===

Welcome, adventurer!

This guide covers the complete skill and magic system.

---PAGE---

=== Skill System ===

- Use skills to gain XP
- +0.1 per successful use
- Max 100 per skill
- Total cap: 700 points

When at skill cap:
Least used skill decreases to make room.

---PAGE---

=== Stats ===

STR (Strength)
- Max HP increase
- Mining speed
- Melee damage

DEX (Dexterity)
- Attack speed
- Movement speed
- Archery accuracy

INT (Intelligence)
- Mana cost reduction
- Spell success rate

=== Crafting Skills ===

Higher skill = better quality!

---PAGE---

=== Stack Size Bonus ===

Production skills increase
max stack size from 64 to 99.

Formula:
64 + (SkillSum / 400 x 35)

Contributing Skills:
- Craftsmanship
- Blacksmithy
- Cooking
- Alchemy

Skill 400 total = 99 stack!

---PAGE---

=== Cooking Skill ===

Effect:
Foods you cook get a
recovery bonus based on
your Cooking skill level.

Quality bonuses:
- LQ: -15% recovery
- NQ: Normal
- HQ: +15% recovery
- EX: +25% recovery

Difficulty varies by food:
- Basic (10): Baked potato
- Medium (25): Soup
- Complex (35): Rabbit stew
- Special (50): Golden items

---PAGE---

=== Alchemy Skill ===

Effect:
Potions you brew get
extended duration based on
your Alchemy skill level.

Skill 100 = +50% duration!

Quality bonuses:
- HQ/EX: Stronger effects

Difficulty varies by potion:
- Basic (30): Healing, Speed
- Enhanced (50): Level II
- Extended (45): 8 min ver
- Splash: +10 difficulty
- Lingering: +20 difficulty

=== Magic System ===

Requirements:

- Reagents (consumed)
- Mana (regenerates)

---PAGE---

=== Getting a Spellbook ===

[Purchase from Librarian]
Librarian villagers sell:
- Empty Spellbook (Lv.3)
- C1/C2 Scrolls (Lv.1-4)

[Learning Spells]
Right-click a scroll to
add the spell to your book.

Scroll sources:
- Librarian villagers
- Magic mobs (Witch, etc)
- End city treasure chests

=== How to Get Runes ===

Mark/Recall/Gate Travel require
a rune item.

[Crafting]
Combine in inventory:
1. Pick up Mark Scroll
2. Click on Amethyst Shard
3. Blank Rune is created!
(Works in either direction)

No skill required, 100% success

Mark Scroll sources:
- Mob drops (Evoker, etc.)
- End City chests
- Craft with /scribe mark

[How to Use]
1. Hold blank rune in hand
2. /cast mark to record location
3. Use marked rune for Recall/Gate

---PAGE---

=== Utility Spells ===

  Reagent: Spider Eye, String

Gate Travel (7th)
  Open portal to rune (30 sec)
  Reagent: Ender Pearl x2, Blaze Powder

---PAGE---

=== Runebook ===

A magical book that stores
multiple runes.

[How to Get]
Purchase from Librarian (Lv.3)
Price: 40-60 emeralds

[How to Use]
1. Right-click to open GUI
2. Drop marked runes to register
3. Store up to 16 runes

[Controls]
- Left-click: Cast Recall
- Shift+Left: Cast Gate Travel
- Right-click: Remove rune

*Normal reagents/mana required

=== Sleep Skip ===

Use /sleep to be counted as
sleeping without a bed.

Requirements:
- Must be night time
- Must be in Overworld
- Someone must already be
  sleeping in a bed

When enough players sleep
(including /sleep users),
night will skip!
