=== Skills Guide ===

Welcome, adventurer!

This guide covers the complete skill and magic system.

---PAGE---

=== Skill System ===

- Use skills to gain XP
- +0.1 per successful use
- Max 100 per skill
- Total cap: 700 points

When at skill cap:
Least used skill decreases to make room.

---PAGE---

=== Stats ===

STR (Strength)
- Max HP increase
- Mining speed
- Melee damage

DEX (Dexterity)
- Attack speed
- Movement speed
- Archery accuracy

INT (Intelligence)
- Mana cost reduction
- Spell success rate

---PAGE---

Weapons:
- Swordsmanship (swords)
- Axe (axes)
- Mace Fighting (maces)
- Archery (bows)
- Throwing (tridents)
- Wrestling (unarmed)

Support:
- Tactics, Anatomy
- Parrying, Focus

---PAGE---

=== Magic System ===

Requirements:
- Spellbook (hold)
- Reagents (consumed)
- Mana (regenerates)
- Magery skill

/cast <spell> to use

---PAGE---

=== Getting a Spellbook ===

[Crafting]
At crafting table:
  Book + Echo Shard
  = Empty Spellbook

[Learning Spells]
Right-click a scroll to
add the spell to your book.

Scroll sources:
- Magic mobs (Witch, etc)
- End city treasure chests

---PAGE---

=== Attack Spells ===

Magic Arrow (1st) - Small dmg
  Reagent: String

Harm (2nd) - Medium dmg
  Reagent: Spider Eye

Fireball (3rd) - Dmg + Fire
  Reagent: Gunpowder, Spider Eye

Lightning (4th) - Large dmg
  Reagent: Gunpowder, Blaze Powder

---PAGE---

=== Attack Spells 2 ===

Mind Blast (5th)
  Drains target's hunger
  Reagent: Spider Eye, Blaze Powder

Explosion (6th) - Area dmg
  Reagent: Gunpowder, Blaze Powder

Meteor Swarm (8th)
  Massive area dmg + fire
  Reagent: Gunpowder, Blaze Powder x3

---PAGE---

=== Healing Spells ===

Heal (1st) - Small HP restore
  Reagent: Golden Carrot

Cure (2nd) - Remove debuffs
  Reagent: Golden Carrot,
           Poisonous Potato

Greater Heal (4th) - Large HP
  Reagent: Golden Carrot x2

---PAGE---

=== Buff Spells ===

Night Sight (1st) - Night vision
  Reagent: Glowstone Dust

Bless (3rd) - Attack power UP
  Reagent: Nether Wart

Protection (5th) - Defense UP
  Reagent: Nether Wart, String

Invisibility (6th)
  Reagent: Nether Wart, Blaze Powder

---PAGE---

=== Movement Spells ===

Teleport (3rd) - Blink to location
  Range: 15 blocks
  Reagent: Ender Pearl

Mark (5th) - Mark a rune
  Reagent: Ender Pearl, Blaze Powder

Recall (5th) - TP to rune
  Reagent: Ender Pearl

---PAGE---

=== Utility Spells ===

Paralyze (5th)
  Immobilize target
  Reagent: Spider Eye, String

Gate Travel (7th)
  Open portal to rune
  Reagent: Ender Pearl x2, Blaze Powder

=== Tips ===

1. Choose a build:
   Warrior, Mage, Crafter

2. 700 cap = specialize

3. Sneak (Shift) to meditate

4. INT reduces mana cost

5. Scrolls use half mana

Good luck!
