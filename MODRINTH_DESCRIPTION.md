# Skills Plugin

A skill-based progression system inspired by classic MMORPGs. Instead of traditional leveling, players develop individual skills through use.

## Key Features

### Skill System
- **37 unique skills** across combat, magic, crafting, gathering, and more
- **700 total skill cap** - specialize or diversify, but you can't master everything
- Skills increase through use and decrease when inactive (if over cap)

### Magic System
- **8 circles of magic** with increasing power and mana costs
- **Spellbook system** - learn spells from scrolls
- **Reagent-based casting** - gather materials to fuel your magic
- **Targeting system** - aim spells at locations or entities

### Spells Include
- Combat: Magic Arrow, Fireball, Lightning, Fire Wall, Meteor Swarm
- Healing: Heal, Cure, Greater Heal
- Buffs: Bless, Protection, Invisibility, Feather Fall, Water Breathing
- Utility: Night Sight, Teleport, Create Food
- Travel: Mark, Recall, Gate Travel (bidirectional portals!)

### Combat System
- Internal health system with STR/DEX/INT stats
- Weapon skills: Swordsmanship, Archery, Fencing, Macefighting
- Defensive skills: Parrying, Wrestling
- Tactical skills: Anatomy (critical hits), Tactics (damage bonus)

### Crafting & Gathering
- Quality tiers from Poor to Legendary based on skill
- Crafting (weapons, armor, tools), Cooking (food, potions), Inscription (scrolls)
- Mining, Lumberjacking, Fishing with bonus drops

### Thief Skills
- Hiding & Stealth movement
- Snooping (peek at inventories)
- Stealing items from players
- Poisoning weapons
- Detecting hidden players

### Taming System
- Tame and control animals
- Animal Lore for creature information
- Veterinary skill to heal your pets

### Additional Features
- **Multi-language support** (English & Japanese)
- **Meditation** for mana regeneration
- **Survival skills** - Athletics, Swimming, Heat Resistance

## Commands

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `/skills` | View your skills |
| `/stats` | View your stats |
| `/cast <spell>` | Cast a spell |
| `/hide` | Attempt to hide |
| `/tame` | Enter taming mode |
| `/lore` | View animal information |

## Requirements

- Minecraft 1.21+
- Paper or Spigot server
